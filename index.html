Lucia Riet de Mahhov

  • Trained psychotherapist with wide-ranging experience in Developmental Psychology, Educational Psychology, Family Counselling, Cognitive and Brief Therapies, Gestalt, Psychoanalysis, Humanistic, Psychodrama, Transactional Analysis and Neuro Linguistic Programming, among others.
  • Recipient of the Fulbright scholarship, she holds a degree in Clinical Psychology and Industrial/Organizational Psychology, post degree in Human Resources Management.
  • Lucia has over 25 years of experience as a trained psycotherapist and life coach. Over the years, she has had clients not only from Estonia but from many different nationalities, including the diplomatic community in Estonia and the international students from Tallinn Technical University.
  • Lucia has vast experience supporting children and families inside and outside the educational environment. Besides her private practice she has worked for more than 15 years as School Psychologist (International School of Estonia and Tallinn English College).
    • Member of the Estonian Association of School Psychologists and of the World Council for Gifted and Talented Children (WCGTC).
  • Originally from South America, she has been living, studying and working in several places, like Montevideo (Uruguay), Denver (USA), London (UK), Saint Petersburg (Russia) and Estonia.
  • She has been a lecturer in Psychology at universities in Uruguay (Universidad D.A. Larrañaga) and Estonia (Tartu University and Concordia International). Has experience in the area of Human Resources having worked at the multinational PriceWaterhouseCoopers and at her own private company in the area of Personnel Selection.
  • Married since 1996, mother of a son and a daughter.
  • Awarded with the Edna McMillan Scholarship in August 2015.
